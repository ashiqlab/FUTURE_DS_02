## 🧩 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes customer retention and churn patterns for a subscription-based business. The dashboard helps businesses understand why customers leave, identify high-risk customer segments, and improve long-term customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn behavior and retention patterns
- Identify high-risk customer groups
- Measure churn rate and customer lifetime
- Improve customer retention strategies

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Active Customers
- Churn Rate
- Average Lifetime
- Monthly Revenue

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Churn by Tenure
- Churn by Payment Method
- Customer Lifetime Distribution
- Plan Type Analysis
- Gender-Based Churn Analysis
- Business Recommendations

---

## 🧠 Insights Gained

- Early-stage customers showed the highest churn
- Electronic check users had higher churn
- Long-term plans improved retention

---
